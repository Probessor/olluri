Common questions

Before you reach out

Do you work with startups outside Norway?

My focus is on the Norwegian ecosystem, but I regularly work with international founders looking to enter the Nordic market, and with non-Norwegian investors seeking Nordic deal flow.

What stage of startups do you work with?

Mostly pre-seed through Series A. The earlier-stage work is typically scouting; later-stage is more investor introductions and market strategy. I'll be upfront if you're outside my sweet spot.

How does your fee structure work?

It depends on the service. Scouting is usually a retainer. Investor matching can be project-based or success-fee. Consulting is hourly or project-based. We'll scope it clearly upfront — no surprises.

Are you available to speak at my event?

Yes, with availability dependent on timing. I speak on Nordic startup ecosystems, venture capital trends, startup scouting, and innovation culture. Send details via the contact form above.

Can I just have a quick chat to see if there's a fit?

Absolutely. That's what discovery calls are for. Tuesdays through Thursdays work best. Use the contact form to request one and I'll follow up to schedule.

Do you invest personally?

No — I'm a scout, not an investor. I don't take equity positions. My role is to connect the right people, not to be a capital allocator myself. This keeps my incentives honest and my advice unbiased.
